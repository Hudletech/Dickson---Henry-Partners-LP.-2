Onyedikachi Dickson Okorie is the Managing Partner at Dickson and Henry Attorneys LP, a leading law firm specializing in aviation law and corporate law. With nearly a decade of professional experience, Dickson has established himself as a highly skilled and trusted legal advisor, delivering strategic solutions to complex corporate and commercial challenges.

A graduate of the prestigious University of Manchester, England, where he earned a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree, Dickson further honed his expertise at Durham University, obtaining a Master of Laws (LLM) in International Trade and Economic Law. His advanced education, combined with his extensive practical experience, has made him a sought-after authority in the fields of aviation law, corporate governance, mergers and acquisitions, regulatory compliance, and cross-border commercial transactions.

Throughout his career, Dickson has earned a reputation for his meticulous attention to detail, analytical acumen, and ability to navigate complex legal landscapes. He has consistently provided innovative legal counsel to both local and international clients, guiding them through the intricacies of corporate law while ensuring the highest standards of governance and legal compliance. His leadership at Dickson and Henry Attorneys LP has contributed significantly to the firm's growth, positioning it as a key player in the legal industry.

Dickson’s commitment to excellence and his proactive approach to legal practice have earned him the respect of clients, colleagues, and industry peers alike. He remains dedicated to advancing corporate law practices and fostering ethical governance standards in today’s rapidly evolving business environment.
